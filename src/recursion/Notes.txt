Recursion - When a function calls itself is known as Recursion
1step - base condition
2step - recursive step


Types of recursion

1.Tail Recursion (calling itself in last statement of a function)
2.Head  Recursion (calling itself in first statement of a function)
3.Linear Recursion (calling itself only one time)
3.Tree Recursion (calling itself more than one time)
4.Indirect Recursion - (more than 1 function calling one another)
5.Nested Recursion (calls itself as a parameter to another invocation of itself)
